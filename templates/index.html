Why

Metronomes improve your sense of beat and tempo. This metronome produces steady beat based on Web Audio API clock.

Metronomes show you where you have technical difficulties. They can be used to gradually speed up the tempo of pieces you are working on or isolate and slow down very difficult passages to work them back to the original speed.

How To

Setting a tempo can be achieved by sliding the weight up and down the pendulum or tapping it on the "Tap" button. To start the metronome click the "Start" button. To stop the metronome click the "Stop" button.

You can control volume with the slider beneath the metronome. The metronome catches on the new volume with a little delay because it has few beats scheduled ahead for steadiness.

Hot keys for BMP increase/decrease are Ctrl-Up Arrow/Ctrl-Down Arrow.
